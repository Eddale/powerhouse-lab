## What We've Learned

**Parallel beats sequential.** The time savings are real and significant. Complex questions should default to swarm, not sequential research.

**Angle selection matters.** Five redundant angles are worse than three distinct ones. The agent needs to pick angles that genuinely cover different ground.

**Synthesis is the hard part.** Individual findings are easy. Combining them into insight requires actual reasoning, not just concatenation.

**Save everything.** Even research that doesn't lead to immediate action is valuable later. The Zettelkasten location ensures findability.

**Video is underused.** YouTube has incredible educational content. The youtube-processor integration makes video-based research practical.
