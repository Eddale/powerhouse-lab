Key Takeaways for Industry

1

Structural Growth is Locked In

Regardless of health improvements, total deaths will rise ~100K annually through 2028. The Baby Boomer demographic wave guarantees increased demand for the next 15-20 years.

2

Watch the Overdose Trend

The 27% decline in overdose deaths (2023-2024) is unprecedented. If sustained, this removes ~30,000 deaths annually from projections. If it reverses, volume increases further.

3

Geographic Variation Matters

States with older populations (Florida, Maine, West Virginia) and those with high obesity rates will see disproportionate growth. Plan capacity accordingly.

4

2030 is the Inflection Point

When deaths exceed births, the cultural and market dynamics shift permanently. Position for this transition now.
